# Audio Strategy

## Goals
- Consistent voice and timing across all devices
- Reliable offline experience
- Reasonable total file size

## Approach
- Record individual technique names (not full combinations)
- Play audio clips sequentially with small gaps
- Use mono audio at 48–56 kbps
- Lazy load audio files by training mode

## Estimated Size
- Target: 550–700 KB total for all audio
- Techniques will be split into folders by mode
